The Model

We Bring the Screen.
You Bring the Space.

OMN provides the complete entertainment infrastructure. Your venue provides the stage. Together we create events that fill your space and delight your audience.

Outdoor Movie Nights Provides

  • → Large LED screens (up to 300") or inflatable screens
  • → HD projectors and full AV rigging
  • → Professional surround sound systems
  • → Full technical crew (setup, live ops, breakdown)
  • → Event concept and creative direction
  • → Digital marketing and community promotion
  • → 71,000+ social following for event promotion
  • → DJs, MCs, and entertainment curation
  • → Backup power and event infrastructure

Your Venue Provides

  • → Outdoor space with sufficient capacity
  • → Venue staff and operational environment
  • → Food & beverage infrastructure
  • → Parking and access management
  • → Existing foot traffic and audience base
  • → Power supply (or generator access)
Partnership first: We validate with low-friction pilots before scaling. You take on no infrastructure costs for the first events.

Choose Your Model

Three Partnership Options

We recommend starting with Option 1 for pilot events, then scaling to Option 2 for larger activations.

Option 1

Venue-Led Model

LOW RISK

Best for pilot events and proof-of-concept. Low risk for both parties. Clear separation of responsibilities.

Revenue Split
Bar & Kitchen: Venue retains 80–90%
Tickets: OMN retains 70–80%
Sponsorship: Shared 50/50
VIP/Lounge: 60/40 (venue-led)
Cost Sharing
Technical setup & screens: OMN
Hospitality & cleaning: Venue
Security: Shared equally
Marketing spend: Shared 50/50
Best for: First 2–3 pilot events — low-risk entry point
RECOMMENDED
Option 2

Equal Partnership

MODERATE RISK

Joint ownership of the event format. All revenue pooled, net profit split 50/50. Maximum alignment of incentives.

Revenue Split
All revenue streams pooled
Net profit split 50/50 after costs
Joint sponsorship acquisition
Equal say in pricing & ticketing
Cost Sharing
All costs shared 50/50
Technical setup: OMN (in-kind)
Venue provision: Partner (in-kind)
Security, DJs, MCs: Joint budget
Best for: Marquee events & large-scale activations · HIGH scalability
Option 3

Production Partner

LOW RISK for OMN

Venue pays OMN a fixed production fee. OMN delivers the technical experience. Venue retains all revenue.

Revenue Split
Venue retains all revenue (bar, kitchen, tickets)
Venue retains all sponsorship & brand deals
OMN receives fixed production fee only
No revenue share — clean, simple arrangement
Cost Sharing
Production fee covers all OMN costs
Venue covers ops, staff, cleaning
Marketing: Venue-led + OMN support
Best for: Venues wanting full operational control
