Soweto Drive-In @ Elkah Stadium

in partnership with NFVF

A landmark moment for OMN — bringing drive-in cinema to Soweto at Elkah Stadium in partnership with the National Film and Video Foundation. Staged during a period when outdoor drive-in formats were in high demand, this event cemented OMN's credibility as an outdoor cinema operator.
