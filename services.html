Outdoor Cinema Nights

Our flagship offering — curated movie nights in parks, precincts, and outdoor venues. We create an immersive cinema atmosphere with colourful bean bags, ambient lighting, professional sound, and a massive screen that fills the night sky.

Perfect for community nights, lifestyle events, and brand experiences. We handle everything from movie licensing to post-event cleanup.

  • Movie selection and licensing
  • Up to 300" inflatable or LED screen

  • Seating, lighting, and décor
